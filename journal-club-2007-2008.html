2007/2008 Journal Club

Cell Motility Journal club is a weekly meeting of PMI faculty and lab members that discusses results from the current scientific literature. Participants explore research related to cell motility, molecular motors, cytoskeleton, muscle biology, and new technologies. Many established scientists can trace their introduction to the critical analysis of scientific literature to this meeting, as it has a rich and lively history that reaches back to the 1960s. All members of the Penn community are invited to attend, discuss, and present! Lunch is provided.
